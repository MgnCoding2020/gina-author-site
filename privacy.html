This privacy policy explains how Little Saporito Press ("we," "us") handles information when you visit littlesaporitopress.com.

What we collect

Email addresses — only if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter. We collect your email and nothing else.

Anonymous traffic data — we use privacy-friendly analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics) to count visits and understand which pages people read. This service does not use cookies and does not collect personal information.

What we don't collect

We don't use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party trackers. We don't sell or share any information.

Newsletter

If you subscribe, your email is stored by our newsletter provider, [Buttondown / ConvertKit / etc. — name to be filled in], and used only to send you updates about new books and releases. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

External links

This site links out to Amazon, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and other services. Once you click a link to one of those services, their own privacy policies apply.

Children's privacy

Although we publish books for children, this website is intended for the adults who buy books for them. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
